# Seed Note: RPG Second Wave Pack

## Purpose

Prepare the second adjunct RPG reflection rollout as a named prep pack without opening a fresh numbered wave.

This pack stages the common second-wave model plus repo-owned ability and feat reflection surfaces on top of the live questbook contour and the first-wave progression / proof spine.

## Why this belongs in Dionysus

`Dionysus` is the seed garden and dispatch layer.

This prep pack belongs here because:

- the rollout spans multiple owning repositories
- the work should remain seed-shaped until the owner repos accept the landings
- first-wave lineage should stay visible instead of being overwritten
- the package is better treated as transport and lineage than as a new authority layer

## Bundle contents to preserve

- repo-local RPG second-wave surfaces for `Agents-of-Abyss`, `aoa-techniques`, and `aoa-skills`
- this `Dionysus` prep-pack note, map, and quest object

## Ownership posture

Read the later plantings under these repo-home boundaries:

- `Agents-of-Abyss` owns the common second-wave model and scope
- `aoa-techniques` owns feat and mastery-harvest reflection
- `aoa-skills` owns ability and loadout reflection
- `Dionysus` owns only seed-garden staging and transport lineage

This pack does not make `Dionysus` the owner of RPG doctrine.

## Scope exclusions

This pack explicitly excludes:

- `aoa-agents`
- `aoa-evals`
- `aoa-playbooks`
- `aoa-memo`
- `aoa-routing`
- `abyss-stack`
- `8Dionysus`
- `aoa-sdk`
- `ATM10-Agent`
- `Tree-of-Sophia`
- `aoa-kag`

## Suggested planting order

Keep the default order flexible but bias toward:

1. `Agents-of-Abyss`
2. `aoa-techniques`
3. `aoa-skills`
4. `Dionysus`

## Final rule

Land the reader-layer contracts before any routing expansion, runtime mechanics, or decorative RPG systems. The second honest win is that live skills can be read as abilities and live techniques can be read as feats without changing who owns their meaning.
