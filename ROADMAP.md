# Dionysus Roadmap

This roadmap is the garden-level direction surface for `Dionysus`.

It is weaker than the live seed surfaces.
If it ever conflicts with the stronger live route, follow these first:

1. the relevant `*_wave.manifest.json` or current live gated seed surface
2. the exact source seed named there
3. the matching closure note when one exists
4. `seed-registry.yaml`

Use this roadmap only after those stronger surfaces to understand the current
garden-level posture and the next cleanup or planting lanes.

## Current phase

`Dionysus` is in post-wave seed-garden stewardship, not in a fresh numbered-
wave expansion pass.

The current posture is:

- archived numbered waves remain lineage roots, not active queue authority
- the current gated next-live seed is `seed_expansion/seed.tos.wider-world-thought-expansion.v0.md#tos-expansion-wider-world-thought-expansion`, still carried as a live seed rather than an opened numbered wave
- live direction is carried by `seed-registry.yaml`, `seed_expansion/`, prep-
  pack notes, and owner-repo reality
- `aoa-sdk` is already landed upstream and should be treated as a live owner
  repo rather than deferred seed separation
- the main open work is keeping registry state, prep-pack lifecycle markers,
  owner-landing trace, and seed-route surfaces truthful as more meaning lands
  upstream

## Current cycle

### Wave 1: live route truth

Goals:

- keep `seed-registry.yaml`, `generated/seed_route_map.min.json`, and the root
  entry docs aligned
- keep `ROADMAP.md` as a garden-level direction door without outranking
  manifests, registry entries, closure notes, or owner reality
- prevent exploratory notes or archived exports from impersonating current
  queue control

### Wave 2: landing followthrough and lineage preservation

Goals:

- keep prep-pack lifecycle markers honest after owner-repo merges
- preserve `reports/planting/` and `docs/SEED_OWNER_LANDING_TRACE.md` only when
  target-repo history would otherwise lose the transplant trail
- keep already-landed repositories treated as live homes once the owner route
  is clear

### Wave 3: prep-pack discipline before any new numbered wave

Goals:

- use named prep packs when flexible future staging is more honest than opening
  another numbered wave
- open a new numbered wave only when prep-pack staging can no longer express
  order, closure, or owner routing cleanly
- keep `Dionysus` small, transplant-focused, and non-sovereign

## Standing discipline

Across all waves:

- keep seed lineage visible
- keep owner repositories explicit
- keep prep packs weaker than live waves and live registry state
- keep repo-local quest followthrough adjunct-only
- do not let the seed garden become a shadow owner of landed repo meaning
