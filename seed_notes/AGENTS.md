# AGENTS.md
Local guidance for `seed_notes/` in `Dionysus`.

Read the root `AGENTS.md` first. This directory may hold exploratory seed notes,
including informal donor, sketch, or reflection surfaces.

## Local role
Notes can preserve early signal, but they are not queue control, not manifest
order, and not owner-repo truth. Promote only the smallest reviewed slice into a
stronger surface when the route is clear.

## Editing posture
Label exploratory posture honestly. Keep donor/provenance hints visible. When a
note becomes planted work, leave trace to the owner repo or stronger Dionysus
surface instead of silently rewriting the note into canon.

## Hard no
Do not let attractive prose bypass manifests, registry state, closure notes, or
owner-repo boundaries.

## Validation
Run:

```bash
python scripts/validate_seed_surfaces.py
```

when notes participate in registry, staging, archive, or planting trace.
