## Purpose

## Contract

The wave closes only when the summon prompt is planted, the service law exists, the mutation and judgment gates are visible, the first receipt is recorded, and the closure note names what remains deferred.

## Acceptance

1. The wave has landed source seeds.
2. Owner repos have readable and structural surfaces.
3. Validation commands are recorded.
4. First summon receipt exists.
5. Deferred work is named without pretending completion.

## Non-goals
