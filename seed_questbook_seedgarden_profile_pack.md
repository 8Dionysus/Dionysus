# Seed Note: Questbook Seed-Garden and Profile Pack

## Purpose

Prepare the final questbook rollout contour from
`archive/seed_pack_exports/questbook_second_wave_seed.zip` for `Dionysus` and
`8Dionysus`.

This pack should come last so the seed garden and profile layer reflect planted
upstream reality instead of racing ahead and becoming a shadow command center.

## Why this belongs in Dionysus

`Dionysus` is the seed garden and dispatch layer.
This prep pack belongs here because:

- both repos are coordination or orientation surfaces, not source-owned doctrine homes
- their questbooks should only track durable follow-through after the upstream questbook contour already exists
- the pack also records that `aoa-sdk` belongs to the next separate named seed after this rollout is complete

## Bundle contents to preserve

The source artifact is `archive/seed_pack_exports/questbook_second_wave_seed.zip`.

It carries:

- `questbook_second_wave_seed/README.md`
- `questbook_second_wave_seed/APPLY_ORDER.md`
- `questbook_second_wave_seed/SEED_MANIFEST.yaml`
- `questbook_second_wave_seed/TOUCHPOINTS_NOT_INCLUDED.md`
- `questbook_second_wave_seed/COVERAGE_MAP.md`
- repo-local questbook starter surfaces for `Dionysus` and `8Dionysus`

## Ownership posture

Read the later plantings under these repo-home boundaries:

- `Dionysus` owns seed-garden protocol, manifests, registry overlays, and durable planting trace
- `8Dionysus` owns public orientation, glossary, and repo-map upkeep

Neither repo should become the source-of-truth owner of specialized AoA, ToS,
KAG, infra, or SDK meaning through questbook rollout.

## Scope exclusions

This pack explicitly excludes:

- `ATM10-Agent`, which is outside this rollout line
- `aoa-sdk`, which should open only as the next separate named seed after these four packs are fully planted
- upstream source/proof and boundary/runtime repos, which should be reflected rather than re-owned here

## Suggested planting order

Keep the default order flexible but bias toward:

1. `Dionysus`
2. `8Dionysus`

## Final rule

Use this pack only after the upstream questbook contour becomes real enough to
mirror honestly. `Dionysus` and `8Dionysus` should stay coordination and
orientation surfaces, not a hidden second sovereignty layer.
