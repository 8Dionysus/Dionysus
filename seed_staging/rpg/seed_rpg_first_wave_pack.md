# Seed Note: RPG First Wave Pack

## Purpose

Prepare the first adjunct RPG reflection rollout as a named prep pack without opening a fresh numbered wave.

This pack stages the common model, agent progression, progression evidence, questline / campaign reflection, chronicle writeback, and derived quest-board seam on top of the live questbook contour.

## Why this belongs in Dionysus

`Dionysus` is the seed garden and dispatch layer.

This prep pack belongs here because:

- the rollout spans multiple owning repositories
- the work should remain seed-shaped until the owner repos accept the landings
- the current live seed should remain intact
- the package is better treated as transport and lineage than as a new authority layer

- this `Dionysus` prep-pack note, map, and quest object

## Ownership posture

Read the later plantings under these repo-home boundaries:

- `Agents-of-Abyss` owns the common model and first-wave scope
- `aoa-agents` owns progression contract
- `aoa-evals` owns progression evidence
- `aoa-playbooks` owns questline and campaign outline
- `aoa-memo` owns chronicle writeback
- `aoa-routing` owns derived quest-board hints
- `Dionysus` owns only seed-garden staging and transport lineage

This pack does not make `Dionysus` the owner of RPG doctrine.

- `8Dionysus`

Keep the default order flexible but bias toward:

1. `Agents-of-Abyss`
2. `aoa-agents`
3. `aoa-evals`
4. `aoa-playbooks`

## Final rule

Land the reflection contracts before any runtime or decorative RPG mechanics. The first honest win is better legibility and bounded progression, not simulated game systems.
