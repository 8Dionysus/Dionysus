# Seed Note: AoA Wave 9 Self-Agency Continuity Pack

## Purpose

Preserve the Wave 9 self-agency continuity layer inside `Dionysus` as landed
lineage.

This pack does not grant autonomous self-modification.
It records the now-landed owner-first continuity family:

- continuity windows
- bounded revision windows
- explicit reanchor decisions
- named anchor-artifact return
- continuity-shaped memo writeback
- derived continuity summaries
- proof of anchor integrity and bounded reflective revision
- the `continuity_ref -> revision_window_ref -> reanchor_ref ->
  anchor_artifact_ref` chain

## Why this belongs in Dionysus

`Dionysus` is the seed garden and dispatch layer.
This pack belongs here because:

- the source artifact spans `Agents-of-Abyss`, `aoa-agents`, `aoa-sdk`,
  `aoa-playbooks`, `aoa-memo`, `aoa-stats`, and `aoa-evals` rather than one
  repo-local patch
- the bundle proposes one new shared continuity vocabulary that must be adapted
  into owner-native contracts instead of copied blindly
- the route must preserve the current owner split between doctrine, agent
  lane, playbook composition, memo support, derived stats, and proof bundles
- the bundle points toward self-agency continuity, but only through explicit
  anchors, review windows, and reanchor posture rather than convenience-memory
  or runtime drift
