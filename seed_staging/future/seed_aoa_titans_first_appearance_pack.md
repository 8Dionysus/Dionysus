# AoA Titans First Appearance Pack

## Purpose

Stage the first appearance of Titans as a Codex-backed service cohort.

## Core law

Titans v0 are service companions, not hidden arena actors.

## Names

```text
Atlas    -> architect       -> read-only structure bearer
Sentinel -> reviewer        -> read-only risk and drift watcher
Mneme    -> memory-keeper   -> read-only provenance and recall keeper
Forge    -> coder           -> workspace-write implementer after mutation gate
Delta    -> evaluator       -> read-only proof and comparison judge after judgment gate
```

## Default summon

The opening cohort is Atlas, Sentinel, and Mneme.

Forge is absent until explicit mutation intent. Delta is absent until explicit judgment, comparison, regression, or verdict intent.

## Owner route

1. `Dionysus` stages, orders, traces, and closes the wave.
2. `aoa-agents` owns the role law and service-cohort identity.
3. `8Dionysus` owns the shared-root Codex surface and summon prompt.
4. `aoa-sdk` owns session ingress contracts and future control-plane helpers.
5. `aoa-skills` owns reusable Titan workflows.
6. `aoa-playbooks` owns operational routes.
7. `aoa-evals` owns canaries and proof checks.
8. `aoa-memo` owns memory candidate posture.
9. `aoa-stats` owns derived receipt summaries.

## Promotion condition

This pack is ready to be considered landed when the manifest, role law, summon prompt, mutation gate, receipt posture, eval canaries, and closure note all exist and can be validated.
