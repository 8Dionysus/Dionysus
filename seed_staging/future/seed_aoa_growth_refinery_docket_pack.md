# Seed Note: AoA Reviewable Growth Refinery Docket Pack

## Purpose

Prepare the next internal AoA wave as one bounded future prep surface inside
`Dionysus`.

This pack preserves a reviewable lineage route from checkpoint carry to
owner-layer landing without pretending the pack itself is already owner truth.

Its center of gravity is:

`cluster_ref -> candidate_ref -> seed_ref -> object_ref`

## Why this belongs in Dionysus

`Dionysus` is the seed garden and dispatch layer.
This prep pack belongs here because:

- the source artifact is a docket bundle, not one owner-repo patch
- the route spans doctrine, control plane, reviewed harvest, seed staging,
  derived observability, proof, playbook method, and memory writeback
- the pack is explicitly about cross-repo lineage, not one repo-local feature
- the uploaded bundle is a staged execution packet that should be adapted into
  owner repos, not copied into them unchanged
- the already-landed Codex access plane is upstream context, not the new work
  headline

## Boundary posture to preserve

Read this pack under these boundaries:

- `Agents-of-Abyss` names doctrine and crosswalks, but does not absorb
  repo-local meaning
- `aoa-sdk` stays control-plane only
- `aoa-skills` owns bounded execution meaning
- `Dionysus` owns seed staging and lineage carry, not final object ownership
- `aoa-stats` stays derived-only
- `aoa-evals` stays proof-only
- `aoa-playbooks` owns recurring multi-surface method
- `aoa-memo` stores explicit memory, not proof
- `aoa-routing` and `aoa-kag` do not become first-authoring homes for this
  route

Keep carry, owner meaning, proof, derived view, and memory writeback as
separate layers.

## Ownership posture

Read later landings under these owner boundaries:

- `Agents-of-Abyss` is the first honest owner for refinery doctrine and
  candidate-lineage crosswalk
- `aoa-sdk` is the first honest owner for provisional lineage carry and
  closeout-control-plane hints
- `aoa-skills` is the first honest owner for reviewed `candidate_ref` minting
- `Dionysus` is the first honest owner for `seed_ref` and seed-stage lineage
  trace
- `aoa-stats` is the first honest owner for the derived funnel summaries
- `aoa-evals` is the first honest owner for lineage integrity, owner-fit, and
  repair-boundedness proof
- `aoa-playbooks` is the first honest owner for the recurring session-growth
  cycle as method
- `aoa-memo` is the first honest owner for failure/recovery memory writeback
- `Dionysus` preserves the pack and its planting posture; it does not move
  final owner truth here

## Application posture

Treat this pack as staged future prep work.

The honest landing order is:

1. `Agents-of-Abyss` doctrine and lineage crosswalk
2. `aoa-sdk` checkpoint and closeout carry for `cluster_ref`
3. `aoa-skills` reviewed donor-harvest candidate contract
4. `Dionysus` seed identity and planting trace
5. `aoa-stats` candidate-lineage and growth-funnel summaries
6. `aoa-evals` lineage and routing proof bundles
7. `aoa-playbooks` session-growth-cycle playbook
8. `aoa-memo` growth-cycle memory writeback seam
9. maturity/status revisit only after real proof and real runs

When planted later, prefer docs + schema + example + validator changes before
aggressive automation, and regenerate repo-owned generated surfaces inside the
repo that owns them.

## What this pack is not for

- no new numbered wave manifest
- no direct multi-repo rollout from this Dionysus staging step
- no `candidate_ref` before reviewed donor harvest
- no `seed_ref` outside `Dionysus`
- no raw `aoa-sdk` checkpoint note logs in `aoa-stats`
- no skipping ahead to proof, playbook, or memo closure while upstream
  contracts are missing
- no authority inflation in `aoa-routing`, `aoa-kag`, `aoa-stats`, or
  workspace glue
- no premature scaffold-to-canonical maturity upgrades

## Final rule

Keep this pack because it names the next real internal AoA route after the
merged Codex-plane landing.

But preserve the difference between:

- one staged future prep pack in `Dionysus`
- one doctrine slice in `Agents-of-Abyss`
- one control-plane carry slice in `aoa-sdk`
- one reviewed candidate contract in `aoa-skills`
- one seed identity slice in `Dionysus`
- one derived funnel slice in `aoa-stats`
- one proof slice in `aoa-evals`
- one recurring method slice in `aoa-playbooks`
- and one later memory writeback slice in `aoa-memo`
