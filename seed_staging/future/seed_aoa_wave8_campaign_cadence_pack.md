# Seed Note: AoA Wave 8 Campaign Cadence Pack

## Purpose

Prepare the next bounded Codex-plane campaign cadence layer as one future prep
pack inside `Dionysus`.

This pack does not replace the existing trusted rollout operations surfaces.
It extends them upward into one reviewable cadence family:

- rollout campaign windows
- drift-review windows
- rollback-followthrough windows
- derived cadence summaries
- bounded cadence-shaped memo examples

## Why this belongs in Dionysus

`Dionysus` is the seed garden and dispatch layer.
This pack belongs here because:

- the source artifact spans `8Dionysus`, `aoa-stats`, `aoa-playbooks`,
  `aoa-memo`, and `aoa-sdk` rather than one repo-local patch
- the bundle proposes one new shared vocabulary layer that must be adapted into
  owner-native contracts instead of copied blindly
- the route must preserve the current owner split between source-owned rollout
  cadence examples, derived stats, playbook companion composition, memo
  writeback, and SDK control-plane posture
- the bundle proposes a new playbook tree that should stay visible as transport
  input only and be adapted into an adjunct under `AOA-P-0028` rather than
  landed as a second sovereign playbook

## Boundary posture to preserve

Read this pack under these boundaries:

- keep `8Dionysus` as the source-owned home for campaign cadence doctrine and
  checked-in example windows that point back to rollout history
- keep `aoa-stats` on derived rollout campaign and drift-review summaries only
- keep `aoa-playbooks` on one companion cadence note under
  `AOA-P-0028 trusted-rollout-operations`, not a new sovereign playbook
- keep `aoa-memo` on bounded drift-review lesson and rollback-followthrough
  pattern writeback using existing memory families
- keep `aoa-sdk` on typed control-plane posture only
- keep landed refs current-date and repo-native instead of preserving pack-local
  lowercase ids

## Ownership posture

Read later landings under these owner boundaries:

- `8Dionysus` is the first honest owner for campaign cadence doctrine,
  lifecycle vocabulary, and checked-in cadence examples
- `aoa-stats` is the first honest owner for derived campaign and drift-review
  summaries
- `aoa-playbooks` is the first honest owner for cadence route composition, but
  only as an adjunct under `AOA-P-0028`
- `aoa-memo` is the first honest owner for cadence-shaped failure-lesson and
  recovery-pattern examples
- `aoa-sdk` is only the boundary-note owner for typed refs versus campaign
  authority
- `Dionysus` preserves staging, archive, and lineage posture only

## Application posture

Treat this pack as staged future prep work.

The honest landing order, if chosen later, is:

1. land `8Dionysus` campaign cadence doctrine plus checked-in example windows

and docs, and rebind example ids to the current planting date `2026-04-12`.

## What this pack is not for

- no new sovereign playbook
- no new rollout scheduler
- no hidden cadence runner

- no memo taxonomy fork for cadence-only objects
- no SDK-local declaration of campaign success or rollback truth

  repo refs
