# Seed Note: AoA Session Harvest Family Prep Pack

## Purpose

Prepare and preserve the `aoa-session-harvest-family` seed as one bounded
lineage surface inside `Dionysus`.

This pack preserves the source bundle that led to the now-landed
post-session growth family around the `aoa-session-donor-harvest` nucleus
without moving live owner truth back into `Dionysus`.

## Why this belongs in Dionysus

`Dionysus` is the seed garden and dispatch layer.
This prep pack belongs here because:

- the source artifact is a seed package, not an owner-repo patch
- the proposal touched several owner layers even though `aoa-skills` was the
  first honest landing surface
- the bundle carries `.seed` files that should be inspected and adapted, not
  copied into live repos unchanged
- the family shape created later technique, eval, playbook, memo, and
  agent-layer consequences that remain important to preserve after planting

## Family posture preserved upstream

Read this pack under these family boundaries:

- keep `aoa-session-donor-harvest` as the nucleus rather than replacing it
- keep `aoa-quest-harvest` narrow as the leaf promotion-triage skill
- treat `route-forks`, `self-diagnose`, `self-repair`, and
  `progression-lift` as explicit siblings rather than hidden widening inside
  `aoa-quest-harvest`
- keep all session-harvest work explicit, post-session, and review-backed
- keep RPG and quest language as reflection layers, not hidden routing
  authority

## Ownership posture

Read later landings under these owner boundaries:

- `aoa-skills` was the first honest owner for the live family landing
- `aoa-techniques` is the home for the extracted technique canon that now
  backs the family
- `aoa-playbooks` may receive later recurring route or repair scenario
  surfaces, but not as first authoring
- `aoa-evals` may receive later diagnosis or progression proof surfaces, but
  not before skill-level meaning is real
- `aoa-memo` may receive later recall or progression writeback surfaces, but
  not as a substitute for reviewed session evidence
- `aoa-agents` may receive later checkpoint or role-boundary law, but not as a
  shortcut around `aoa-skills`
- `aoa-routing` and `aoa-kag` remain derivative consumers, not first-authoring
  homes
- `Dionysus` only preserves the pack and its planting posture; it does not move
  owner truth here
