# Seed Note: AoA Antifragility Fourth-Wave Prep Pack

## Purpose

Prepare the `aoa-antifragility-fourth-wave-seed` bundle as one bounded
proof-and-consumption prep pack inside `Dionysus`.

This note now preserves that fourth-wave family as landed lineage, including
the original prep posture and the later owner-repo merges, without turning
`Dionysus` into the owner of proof, derivation, routing authority, or memory
truth.

## Why this belongs in Dionysus

`Dionysus` is the seed garden and dispatch layer.
This prep pack belongs here because:

- the source artifact is a repo-relative overlay seed, not a live proof and
  derivation rollout
- the original staging bundle depended on first-wave receipts, second-wave
  transport seams, and third-wave behavior surfaces before the owner-repo
  landings refreshed those layers upstream
- the package spans proof, derived stats, routing consumption, and reviewed
  recall at once
- the original owner-repo reality check found no current antifragility traces
  in `aoa-evals`, `aoa-stats`, `aoa-routing`, or `aoa-memo` before the later
  merges
- the later-wave note should stay lineage-only even after the owner landings
  merge

## Boundary posture to preserve

Read this pack under these boundaries:

- keep `aoa-evals` on bounded proof rather than workflow ownership
- keep `aoa-stats` derived and evidence-linked rather than sovereign
- keep `aoa-routing` additive and subordinate to owner receipts plus proof
- keep `aoa-memo` reviewed and contextual rather than proof or live health
- keep repeated-window quality split across axes rather than one prestige
  scalar
- keep hidden repair, hidden re-entry, hidden republishing, and prestige
  scoring out of scope

## Ownership posture

Read later landings under these owner boundaries:

- `aoa-evals` owns repeated-window proof posture and report shape
- `aoa-stats` owns derived recovery-window summaries only
- `aoa-routing` owns additive composite route hints
- `aoa-memo` owns reviewed recovery-pattern memory and recall posture
- `Dionysus` only preserves the bundle and its sequencing posture

Treat this pack as landed fourth-wave lineage, not as a staged-only queue
marker.

## What this pack is not for

- no immediate proof-and-consumption rollout from `Dionysus`
- no bypass around earlier-wave owner boundaries
- no centralization of stress meaning in evals, stats, routing, or memo
- no routing overrule of owner evidence
- no memo treatment as live health truth
- no one-number recovery prestige score

## Final rule

Keep this pack because it names the later proof, derivation, routing, and
recall seams that now sit on top of an honest earlier-wave antifragility
stack.

But preserve the difference between:

- proof-owned verdicts
- derived summaries
- additive route hints
- reviewed recall context
- one preserved fourth-wave pack in `Dionysus`
